# Finn Johansson

## Education
Finn's artistic education began with a traditional apprenticeship under master ice sculptor Lars Nielsen. He learned the ancient craft of working with ice and snow, eventually developing his own unique style.

## Work History

### Owner - Frostbite Creations (2015-Present)
Specializes in large-scale ice sculpture for:
- Winter festivals
- Corporate events
- Public art installations
- Known for intricate details and stunning, ephemeral beauty

### Assistant Sculptor - Arctic Arts Festival (2010-2015)
Contributed to some of the world's most impressive ice and snow constructions:
- Gained valuable experience in large-scale art installation
- Developed project management skills
- Worked on internationally recognized projects

## Skills & Passions
Finn's work is his passion, and he thrives on the challenge of creating beautiful art from a temporary medium.

### Technical Expertise
- Expert in advanced ice carving and shaping techniques
- Deep understanding of thermal physics
- Knows how to manipulate ice to achieve artistic vision
- Master of working with ephemeral materials

### Creative Philosophy
Creates art that is inherently temporary, finding beauty in the fleeting nature of ice sculptures.

### Personal Interests
His love for homebrewing complements his artistic work:
- Sees brewing as a similar blend of science and art
- Precise measurements combined with creativity
- Both crafts require patience and attention to detail
- Results in unique and satisfying creations
