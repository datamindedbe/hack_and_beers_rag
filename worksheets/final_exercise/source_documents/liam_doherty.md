# Liam Doherty

## Education
Liam received his certification as a Wilderness Survival Instructor from The Mountain Leadership Institute, an organization renowned for its rigorous training programs.

## Work History

### Owner & Lead Guide - Wildwood Expeditions (2015-Present)
Specializes in multi-day wilderness treks in the remote areas of British Columbia:
- Teaches clients everything from basic navigation to advanced survival techniques
- Fosters a deep respect for the natural world
- Provides comprehensive wilderness education

### Forest Ranger - Parks Canada (2008-2015)
Responsibilities included:
- Trail maintenance
- Search and rescue operations
- Educating park visitors on safety and conservation
- Wildlife management and protection

## Skills & Passions
Liam's passion is sharing his deep knowledge of the wilderness with others.

### Professional Skills
- Expert in wilderness navigation
- First aid and emergency response certified
- Wide range of survival techniques
- Skilled woodworker - crafts tools and furniture from raw materials

### Personal Philosophy
His work reflects his belief that by connecting with nature, we learn more about ourselves and our place in the world. He sees wilderness education as both practical training and spiritual development.

### Craftsmanship
In addition to his wilderness expertise, Liam is a skilled woodworker who uses his hands to create functional items from natural materials.
