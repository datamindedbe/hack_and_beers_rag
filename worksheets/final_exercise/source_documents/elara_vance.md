# Elara Vance

## Education
Elara holds a Master's in Astrophotography from the prestigious University of the Stars, an institution uniquely located in a remote part of the Himalayas. Her thesis focused on ancient star charts and their surprising accuracy in predicting celestial events.

## Work History

### Celestial Cartographer (2022-Present)
As a self-employed Celestial Cartographer, Elara combines her love of ancient traditions with modern technology. Her work involves:
- Charting new constellations and tracking planetary movements
- Providing data to astronomical research centers
- Creating intricate, hand-drawn maps for private collectors
- Working on commission to document specific celestial events like meteor showers and comets

### Research Assistant (2018-2022)
At the Institute for Himalayan Astronomy, she:
- Helped catalog new astronomical discoveries
- Maintained the observatory's equipment

## Skills & Passions
Elara's deep passion lies in bridging the gap between ancient knowledge and modern science. Her unique skill set includes:
- Advanced digital astrophotography
- Precision drafting
- Rendering star maps with scientific accuracy and artistic beauty
- Expert knowledge of ancient astronomical texts and their interpretation

Her work gives her a profound sense of history, combining ancient wisdom with modern scientific methods.
