# Jun-ho Park

## Education
Jun-ho holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Fashion Design from Seoul National University. His time there exposed him to both traditional Korean aesthetics and modern architectural principles, which would later inspire his work.

## Work History

### Founder & Head Designer - Jun-ho Park Studio (2020-Present)
Has carved out a niche in the high-end fashion world:
- Collections known for sculptural silhouettes
- Thoughtful blend of historical and contemporary influences
- Recognized for innovative design approach
- Builds exclusive, high-quality fashion pieces

### Senior Designer - The House of Jin (2016-2020)
Gained valuable experience in the commercial side of the industry:
- Production management
- Trend forecasting
- Commercial fashion development
- Team leadership and collaboration

## Skills & Passions
Jun-ho's work is his passion, and he approaches fashion as both art and storytelling.

### Technical Expertise
- Expert in fashion design and illustration
- Master of pattern making
- Skilled in textile selection and manipulation
- Deep understanding of garment construction

### Artistic Vision
- Designs are a form of storytelling
- Creates clothing that is both beautiful and meaningful
- Perfectionist who pushes fabric possibilities
- Exceptional eye for detail

### Cultural Integration
His work thoughtfully combines:
- Traditional Korean aesthetics
- Modern architectural principles
- Contemporary fashion trends
- Historical fashion influences

Each piece represents a narrative that bridges cultures and time periods.
