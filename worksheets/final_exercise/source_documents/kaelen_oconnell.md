# Kaelen O'Connell

## Education
Kaelen completed a rigorous five-year apprenticeship with the British Horological Institute, earning his diploma and becoming a master clockmaker. He also attended workshops on the restoration of antique timepieces, an area in which he excels.

## Work History

### Owner & Proprietor - O'Connell's Clock Repair (2005-Present)
Located in Dublin, Kaelen's business is a cherished local landmark. He specializes in:
- Restoration of antique clocks
- Working on everything from delicate pocket watches to large-scale church clocks
- Serving as a custodian of history through his repair work

### Apprentice - Dublin Timepiece Guild (1995-2005)
Kaelen began his career learning the intricate craft from some of Ireland's finest horologists.

## Skills & Passions
His work is his greatest passion. Kaelen finds immense satisfaction in:
- The meticulous, almost meditative process of disassembling broken clocks
- Bringing intricate mechanisms back to life
- Expert mechanical clock and watch repair
- Exceptional metalworking and fine motor skills
- Deep knowledge of historical timepieces

He appreciates the craftsmanship and stories behind each clock, often sharing these with his customers.
