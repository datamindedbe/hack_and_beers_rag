# Chloe Dubois

## Education
Chloe earned her Master of Landscape Architecture from the prestigious École Nationale Supérieure de Paysage in Versailles. Her studies emphasized:
- Sustainable design principles
- Social impact of green spaces
- Environmental stewardship

## Work History

### Founder & Principal - Dubois & Associates (2020-Present)
Designs public parks, community gardens, and sustainable residential landscapes:
- Projects focus on natural materials and biodiversity
- Creates spaces that encourage social interaction
- Emphasizes community-centered design

### Senior Designer - GreenSpaces Urban Planning (2015-2020)
Worked on several large-scale municipal projects:
- Led the revitalization of an abandoned waterfront into a public park
- Gained experience in large-scale urban planning
- Developed expertise in municipal project management

## Skills & Passions
Chloe's work is driven by a strong belief that everyone deserves access to beautiful, functional green spaces.

### Professional Expertise
- Sustainable design specialist
- Project management
- CAD software proficiency
- Municipal planning experience

### Creative Vision
- Expert in horticulture
- Creates thriving, natural environments that are both beautiful and resilient
