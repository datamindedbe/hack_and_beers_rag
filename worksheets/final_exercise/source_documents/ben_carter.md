# Ben Carter

## Education
Ben holds a B.A. in Criminology from the University of Manchester. The knowledge he gained during his studies provided a strong foundation for his long and distinguished career in law enforcement.

## Work History

### Crime Novelist (2020-Present)
Since retiring from the force, Ben has found a new outlet for his experience as a crime novelist:
- Authored three successful crime novels
- Known for gritty realism and complex characters
- Draws on extensive knowledge of police procedures and criminal psychology

### Detective Inspector - Greater Manchester Police (1985-2019)
Over his 34-year career, Ben:
- Served as lead investigator on numerous high-profile cases
- Honed skills in criminal investigation, forensic analysis, and interview techniques
- Built a distinguished record in law enforcement

## Skills & Passions
Ben's passion is storytelling. He sees his novels not just as entertainment but as a way to:
- Explore the human condition
- Examine the ethical complexities of justice
- Continue his investigative work through fiction

### Professional Skills
- Advanced research capabilities
- Encyclopedic knowledge of criminal history
- Deep understanding of human nature
- Expert in investigative techniques

His work is a direct continuation of his career, applying his investigative mind to fictional narratives.
