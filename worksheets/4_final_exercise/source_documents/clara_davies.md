# Clara Davies

## Education
Clara received her Master of Library Science from the University at Albany, SUNY. Her education instilled in her a deep respect for books and the power of knowledge.

## Work History

### Head Librarian - Greenfield Public Library (1980-2015)
Served for 35 years, transforming the library into a community hub:
- Managed the library's collection and cataloging system
- Organized community events and programming
- Oversaw a staff of a dozen employees
- Under her leadership, the library became a beloved community center

### Assistant Librarian (1975-1980)
Began her career focusing on:
- Cataloging and information organization
- Patron assistance and reference services
- Foundation building in library science

## Current Activities
Even in retirement, Clara continues to:
- Volunteer at the library
- Share her knowledge and love of books with new generations
- Mentor young librarians
- Support literacy initiatives

## Skills & Passions
Clara's career was a testament to her passion for reading and public service.

### Professional Expertise
- Expert in information management
- Master of cataloging systems
- Community programming specialist
- Staff management and leadership

### Community Impact
- Passionate about literacy promotion
- Worked tirelessly to make the library accessible and welcoming for everyone
- Believed in the democratizing power of free access to information
- Champion of lifelong learning

Her legacy is a library that serves not just as a repository of books, but as a vital community resource for education, culture, and connection.
